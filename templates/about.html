About Personal Expense Tracker

Your comprehensive personal finance management solution

Application Overview

Personal Expense Tracker is a powerful, user-friendly web application designed to help you take complete control of your finances. Built with modern web technologies, it provides a comprehensive suite of tools for tracking, analyzing, and managing your personal finances.

Built With:

Python Flask SQLite Database HTML5 & CSS3 JavaScript & Chart.js Bootstrap Icons

Key Features

Transaction Management

Easily add, edit, and categorize your income and expenses with our intuitive interface.

Visual Reports

Beautiful charts and graphs to visualize your spending patterns and financial trends.

Category Management

Organize your expenses with customizable categories for better financial organization.

Budget Tracking

Set budgets for different categories and get alerts when you're approaching limits.

Advanced Analytics

Deep insights into your spending habits with personalized recommendations and trends.

Smart Search

Quickly find any transaction with powerful search and filtering capabilities.

Recurring Transactions

Automate regular income and expenses like salaries, rent, and subscriptions.

Data Export

Export your financial data to CSV format for external analysis or backup.

Security & Privacy

Secure Authentication

Password-protected user accounts with secure session management.

Data Isolation

Your financial data is completely private and isolated from other users.

Local Storage

All data is stored locally on your device for maximum privacy and security.
